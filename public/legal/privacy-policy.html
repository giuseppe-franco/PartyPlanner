Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 4, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal data is collected, used, and handled when you use the Mobile Team Winter Celebration platform ("Service"). This Service, created as a personal initiative, manages food and drink contributions, photo sharing, and feedback collection for the event.

2. Data Collection and Usage

2.1 Food and Drink Registration Data

We collect and process:

  • Your name
  • Item description (food or drink you're bringing)
  • Category of item
  • Any notes (including allergy information)
  • Timestamp of registration

This information becomes read-only when the event starts.

2.2 Photo Sharing Data

After event start, we collect:

  • Photos you upload
  • Your name as photo uploader
  • Upload timestamps

2.3 Feedback Data

Three hours after event start, we collect:

  • Your name (if provided)
  • Feedback messages
  • Reactions to feedback
  • Feedback timestamps

3. Purpose of Data Collection

Your data is used for:

  • Coordinating food and drink contributions for the event
  • Displaying and sharing event photos among attendees
  • Collecting and sharing event feedback
  • Ensuring proper Service functionality

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

  • All data is stored using Google Firebase secure infrastructure
  • Access is limited to event attendees only
  • Different retention periods apply to different types of data
  • No data is shared with third parties

5. Data Retention Periods

5.1 Food and Drink Registration

This data is:

  • Active and editable until event start
  • Read-only after event start
  • Deleted after event completion

5.2 Photos and Associated Data

  • Stored for 10 days after the event
  • Automatically deleted after retention period
  • No backups maintained after deletion

5.3 Feedback Data

  • Available three hours after event start
  • Stored alongside photo data
  • Deleted with photo data after 10 days

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your content (photos, feedback)
  • Modify your food/drink registration (before event start)
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Data portability

7. Automated Processing

The Service performs the following automated processing:

  • Automatic transition to read-only mode for food/drink registration at event start
  • Automatic enabling of photo sharing at event start
  • Automatic enabling of feedback after three hours
  • Automatic deletion of content after retention period

8. Third-Party Services

The Service uses:

  • Google Firebase for hosting and data storage
  • OpenWeather API for weather information display

These services are used solely for Service functionality and not for tracking or marketing purposes.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

Updates to this policy will reflect:

  • Service improvements
  • Legal and regulatory requirements
  • User feedback and concerns

Users will be notified of any material changes.

10. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, please contact the Service creator through the Mobile Team Winter Celebration organizers.

11. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Your consent when registering items or uploading content
  • Legitimate interests in coordinating event contributions
  • Compliance with EU GDPR regulations
